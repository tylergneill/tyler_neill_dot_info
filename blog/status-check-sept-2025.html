Status Check (mini-post)

September 15, 2025

It's been almost five months since I last published anything on the blog — sorry for the radio silence! There's been a lot of interconnected work to get through, but I'm just about ready to start sharing again.

A brief programmatic note: I originally imagined this blog as a space where I would mostly learn from and write about others' projects, and only occasionally give updates on my own research. In practice, it's turned out the other way around. I currently have two or three more posts of that latter type in the works, hopefully dropping soon. Over time, I expect the balance to shift back, but we'll see.

  • Wisdom Library (Gabe Hiemstra)
  • Jain eLibrary (Anish Visaria)

And some topics:

If you other ideas, please let me know! Talk more soon.
