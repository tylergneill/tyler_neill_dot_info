Kalpataru Grove

2024

Kalpataru Grove is a curated collection of digital Sanskrit resources, bringing together various tools and materials in one accessible place. The name refers to the mythical "wish-fulfilling tree" (kalpataru) - the idea being that this grove of resources can help fulfill the wishes of Sanskrit learners and researchers.

What's Included

The collection includes links to dictionaries, grammar resources, text repositories, digital tools, and learning materials. Rather than being a comprehensive encyclopedia, it's a curated selection of resources I've found genuinely useful.
