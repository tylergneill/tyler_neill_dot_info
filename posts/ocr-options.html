OCR Options

April 2024

Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for Sanskrit and Devanagari texts has come a long way. This post surveys the main options available for converting scanned Sanskrit documents into editable text.

Available Tools

Several OCR solutions work with Devanagari script:

  • Google Cloud Vision / Google Drive: Surprisingly effective for printed Devanagari. Upload a PDF to Google Drive and open with Google Docs.
  • Tesseract: Open-source OCR engine with Devanagari support. Available through Skrutable.
  • Sanskrit OCR (IHDIA): Specialized model trained specifically on Sanskrit texts.

Practical Tips

OCR quality depends heavily on scan quality. Clean, high-resolution scans of modern printed texts will give much better results than low-quality scans of old books or manuscripts.

For quick OCR of Devanagari images, you can use the OCR feature in Skrutable.
